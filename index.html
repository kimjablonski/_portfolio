ABOUT ME

I am Kim Jablonski, a graphic designer living in Menomonie, Wisconsin. I specialize in print, communication and interactive design. I have experience with point of purchase displays, 3D renderings, creating logos/brand identities, print designs and html/css coding.

As a designer I am inspired by the world around me, anything from people, processes or the natural world. I strive to learn the bigger picture and how things work outside my specific job. I pride myself on being willing and able to learn about more than just my piece of the puzzle.

I thrive in workplaces that are individual based as well as collaborative and inspire creative thinking. I aim to meet, collaborate and work with other professionals in design fields in hopes to increase my skill and knowledge, while still bringing something new to the table.

Aside from graphic design, I am passionate about nature and animals including my 4 dogs and 4 turtles. For as long as I can remember I have been a member of a softball, basketball or volleyball team. I love to be active, especially when it involves being outside.
